Founder & CEO

Aaron Hennings

Aaron founded Trailside Risk Partners on a simple belief: insurance should be a relationship, not a transaction. He built the firm to put clients first — independent by design, so the only interests on the table are yours.

He brings rare technical breadth to that promise: CRM (Certified Risk Manager), CIC (Certified Insurance Counselor), CRIS (Construction Risk & Insurance Specialist), and CWCA (Certified WorkComp Advisor). Every recommendation is grounded in real expertise across commercial, construction, and workers' compensation.

Most of all, Aaron is a hands-on advocate — he maps exposures, closes gaps, and stands beside clients through every claim, so you can move forward with confidence.
